Today, 7 April 2023, the All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS) will end the registration period for the AIIMS Institute of National Importance Super Specialty Entrance Test (INI SS) for July 2023. Through the official website, aiimsexams.ac.in candidates can submit an application for the AIIMS INI SS July 2023. The AIIMS INI SS July 2023 exam admit card will be released on 21 April 2023. The examination will take place on 29 April 2023, for a duration of 90 minutes. There will be 80 questions in total in the examination.
